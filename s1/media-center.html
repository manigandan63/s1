At MSD, we are inspired by a shared vision and mission to improve and save lives.

We’re a global healthcare company working to make a difference in the lives of people through the innovative medicines, vaccines, and consumer health and animal products that we discover and produce.

Our core therapeutic areas include diabetes, cancer, vaccines, women’s health, asthma, cholesterol, hypertension and hospital acute care. We continue to focus our research on conditions that represent some of today’s most significant health challenges – like cancer, hepatitis C, cardio-metabolic disease, antibiotic-resistant infection and Alzheimer's disease, and we are on the front lines in the fight against emerging global pandemics, such as Ebola. We are also building strengths in new areas like biologicals.

At MSD, we're applying our global reach and scientific excellence to do more of what we're passionate about: improving health and saving lives.
